Welcome to my blog. This is where I'll share my thoughts on technology, programming, and whatever else captures my interest.

I believe in the ethos of learning by building. Most of what I actually understand came from trying to make something work, breaking it along the way, and figuring out why. This blog is partly a record of those attempts and partly a way to keep myself honest about what I do and do not understand yet.

Starting a blog has been on my list for a while. There's something valuable about writing down your thoughts. It forces you to clarify your thinking and creates a record you can look back on.

## What to Expect

I plan to write about:

- Tools and workflows that I find useful
- Data Centers
- HPC 
- Distributed Computing
- Lessons learned from projects
- Interesting problems and their solutions
- Personal Interests 

Thanks for stopping by. Stay tuned for more posts.
